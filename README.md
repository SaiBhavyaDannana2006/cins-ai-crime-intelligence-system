## Overview

CINS (Crime Intelligence & Nexus System) is a centralized intelligence management platform designed to simulate modern investigative workflows used in law enforcement and criminal analysis environments.

The system focuses on managing interconnected criminal data through structured relational mapping between suspects, cases, officers, evidence records, and civilian intelligence.

Unlike traditional CRUD-based management systems, CINS emphasizes investigative relationships, operational visibility, and intelligence-oriented data organization.
